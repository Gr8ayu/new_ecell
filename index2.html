ABOUT E-CELL

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) of RVCE, founded in 2007 stands tall as a student-run body with the goal of fostering the Entrepreneurial culture within the college campus, and beyond. The Cell stands out as a pioneering organization, having mentored myriad Startups over the years. We at E-Cell, also provide opportunities for our members to widen their network within the Entrepreneurial world through the many events and speaker sessions that we organize.

MISSION

Our mission of developing the change makers of the society is achieved by organizing seminars, competitions, workshops, conferences and these will impart the relevant skills to an entrepreneur, to anyone willing to take the step
