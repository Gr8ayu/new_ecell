About E-Cell

The Entrepreneurship Cell, RVCE, founded in 2007 stands tall as a student-run body with the goal of fostering an entrepreneurial culture and providing the most unique opportunities to the student community. The Cell stands out as a pioneering organization having mentored a myriad of startups over the years. We at E-Cell also provide opportunities for our members to gain a better understanding of the startup community by facilitating regular interactions with them.

Our Initiatives


ESUMMIT

E-summit is an annual celebration of Entrepreneurship. With a massive footfall of over 8k,
it is a summit for budding entrepreneurs, students, professionals, mentors, angel investors and venture
capitalists.There is a stunning array of competitions for students to showcase their talents. Workshops are
conducted to ensure that startups acquire the necessary skills to supplement their dreams.


EMBARK

Embark is held each year as the inaugural event of the cell. It’s an idea and innovation workshop open
to all students of RVCE. The focus is on brainstorming, idea generation and Business-Plan
creation. They are judged by a panel of experts from various fields.


Gyaan Session

Entrepreneurs are invited from various ﬁelds to encourage students in the path of entrepreneurship.
It also helps students gain a better perspective into the world of entrepreneurs by
continuous interaction with these experts.


EXPEDIENS

Expediens is a Business Plan competition that provides an opportunity for students to obtain
mentorship and investment. It is a flagship event of the cell and has witnessed participants coming in
from different parts of the country.


RVCR

RVCR is a Campus Ambassador program which facilitates students from other colleges to work
closely with our Cell. It offers numerous benefits as it helps students gain a better understanding of the
Cell’s activities and gain access to the community we have helped create over the years.


Envisioner

Envisioner is a monthly magazine that takes our endeavours one step ahead and aims to create a better
understanding of the changing trends and the world around us.
