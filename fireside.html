Fireside Chat

Fireside Chat

Event details

The event has been annually conducted having very important speakers to shed light on crucial topics in the form of a panel discussion. This year we were proud to host Subbu Raghunathan, Director of Payment Engineering at PayPal and Pawas Chandra, , director at Wework as our panel members along with an Alumnus Moderator, Prakhar Jain who graduated from ECE in 2018.

The Topic of Discussion was decided to be ‘Adaptation and Evolution in the transient Markets’, for which the panel had a lot to contribute. The discussion started with the introduction of the panel members by the EmCees and then by the moderator at 11:30am. The moderator then asked each of them to brief their view on the topic for about five minutes, followed by a series of questions directed to both the members which was answered strategically and their thoughts on aspects such as ‘changing for competition’ was very insightful. The panel discussion was carried out for 45 minutes after which a small Q and A session of about 10 minutes was held, with three people predominantly asking the questions.

At the end of the successful event, the panel members and the moderator were given mementos for taking time and coming to our college at around 12:30pm and finally were accompanied till they left the premises.
