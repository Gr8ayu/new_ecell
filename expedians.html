Expedians

Instated to foster the spirit of entrepreneurship and business acumen among people all around the country, Expediens was a B-plan competition in E-SUMMIT'19 that narrowed down the finest start-ups and student ideas from a plethora of teams participating across the nation. Expediens provided a platform for potential ideas and early stage start-ups to evaluate their plan in the risk-free environment of a competition. Right from acknowledging that the idea had potential, to drafting a B-plan and pitching in front of an esteemed panel of investors, this platform had it all! It aimed to break the barriers between ideas and the realization of an enterprise.

Event details

Expediens consisted of 3 Rounds. The first round was to register for the competition by submitting the idea for pitching, on a Google form. The teams could have a maximum number of 5 people. The registered ideas were scrutinised, and the ones selected for the next round were intimated through mail.

The second round was the Ideation Round wherein each team had to present their idea in front of the judges within 3 minutes. This round of competition happened on the 22nd of February in the Biotech and MCA Seminar Halls. The panel of judges consisted of:

Mr. Bhaskar M

Mr. Srinath M R

Mr. G Nagendra

Mr. Anoop B S

Based on the evaluated scores, 14 teams were selected for the final round, that is the Business Pitch Round. This round was held on the 23rd of February, at the MCA Seminar Hall. Teams were given 8 minutes to present their business plan in front of a panel that included venture capitalists. The following were in the panel of judges:

Ms. Sukriti Saroj

Mr. Abhishek Appaji

Mr. Jayanth P Bellur

There were two categories in which prizes were allotted: Startup and Student Idea.
The standings at the end of the competition were:
Winners (Startup)- FabGuard (Mumbai)
Runners (Startip)- Pincava (Bangalore)
Winners (Student)- Suhas B U & Harini V (RVCE)
Runners (Student)- Kritharth Mittal (SRM Chennai)

Expediens 2019 was deemed successful in terms of the quality of the ideas and number of startup registrations received. The organizing was lauded for the way in which the competition was carried out over the two days with respect to the time management and event flow.
